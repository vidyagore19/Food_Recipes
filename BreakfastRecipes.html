“ Starting your day with a nutritions breakfast kick-starts your metabolism and provides fuel for your Body and Brain.” – Lena Bean

- Whether you are enjoing a leisurely Sunday morning or getting ready for work, it is important to take time to eat breakfast.By Skipping breakfast, you miss out an important nutrients that you will not make up for later in the day.

-A study by the American journal of Epidemiology found that people who skipped breakfast were almost five times more likely to be obese.And recent study from the journal of the American dietetic association found that children and adolescents who skipped breakfast had a higher body mass index-for-age and a higher waist circumference than those who ate breakfast.

-The whole meal should total between 300 to 400 calories for example,eat 1/2 cup of wheat cereal,1/2 cup of blueberries or 1 small banana and 1/2 cup of low-fat milk.



-Breakfast Recipes-


1. Sandwich

Step-1:Wash and peel 300 grams potatoes.i prefer to quarter them and steam over a pot just until done.you can also boil the whole potatoes in a pressure cooker for 3 wistles or 5 to 9 minutes in the instant pot depending on the size of your potatoes.

step-2:to a mixing bowl, add 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger 1 green chilli,chopped coriander leaves,1/2 tablespoon red chilli powder,1/4 tablespoon salt,1/8 teaspoon termeric powder,1/4 or 1/2 teaspoon garam masala,1/2 teaspoon coriander powder.pour 1 to tablespoon ghee or oil over the spices.

step-3:Mic up everything.you can also add lemon juice or amchur powder now.

step-4:Add mashed potatoes .mix up everything well.taste test and add more spices,salt or lemon juice to taste.

step-5:Smear softned butter or ghee on one side of the bread.I prefer ghee.turn the bread slice to the other sid.if you want you can also smear some green chutney or shezwan sauce and then spread the aloo masala.cover with the another slice and smear butter or ghee in the outer side.you can also smear chutney or sauce on both the slices.

step-6:Heat the sandwich griller and grill the sandwich for 3 to 5 minutes until crisp.alternative you can also toast them on a hot griddle on both the sides until crisp and golden.

step-6:Cut aloo sandwich to squares or tringles and serve with green chutney or shezwan sauce.

2. Kanda Poha

step-1:Pick 1.5 cups poha.Rinse them in clean running water.then keep that for 10-15 minuts.

step-2:Take 1/4 teaspoon termeric powder,1 teaspoon sugar and salt accordingly to taste on the rinsed poha flakes.Gently mix.

step-3:take dry roast 2 tablespoon peanuts in a heavy pan on medium low heat until they become crunchy.then keep it one plate.

step-4:take some oil in the kadai,add 1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds and heat it gently.then add cumin seeds

step-5:then add one finely choped onion and heat until onion become transparent then add 8-9 curry leaves ,1 spoon choped green chilies and roasted peanute.mix all with low heat.

step-6:then add poha and mix well this mixture.then add one boiled potato.

step-7:add sugar and sold as you like then mix all mixture and then cover the pan tightly with lid and steam the poha for 2 minuts on low flame.

3. Idali

step-1:Add 1/2 cup dal to a bowl and wash few times until water is clear.pour fresh water and soak for about 6 hour.Add one cup rice in another boul and wash it.and soak rice for about 6 hour.

step-2:To make Batter:
after 6 hours,drain off the water from the dal and rice and then add solt and make fine mixture.

step-3:Ferment batter:
Mix well the mixture.keep it aside for fermenting in warm place for 6-12 hour ,depeding upon the climate.

step-4:After Fermentation the batter has to double and tern light ,fluffy and bubbly.gently mix the batter,very lightly to make it uniform.

step-5:Steam Idali:
Bring water to boil in steamer or pressure cooker without weight on the medium flame.grease the idaly plates.pour that mixture in the molds.

step-6:When water begin to bubble up rapidly, place the idaly stand in the streamer.and steam it for 20 minutes.

step-7:After 20 minutes, loose the idali from the plates with the help of spoon.

step-8:Add some Ghee and serve with Coriander coconut chutney or Peanut Chutney.

4. Dosa

step-1:Add 1/2 cup dal to a bowl and wash few times until water is clear.pour fresh water and soak for about 6 hour.Add one cup rice in another boul and wash it.and soak rice for about 6 hour.

step-2:To make Batter:
after 6 hours,drain off the water from the dal and rice and then add solt and make fine mixture.

step-3:Ferment batter:
Mix well the mixture.keep it aside for fermenting in warm place for 6-12 hour ,depeding upon the climate.

step-4:After Fermentation the batter has to double and tern light ,fluffy and bubbly.gently mix the batter,very lightly to make it uniform.

step-5:Heat a cast iron pan.When the pan becomes hot, spread some oil on pan with spoon. you can use Ghee or butter for more taste.keep the flame medium.

step-6:Now take the batter and spread on the pan.then cover the pan.cook till the base is nicely golden and crisp.then tern the dosa and cook upper side of dosa for some time.

step-7:Serve Dosa with Coriander coconut chutney or Peanut Chutney and Enjoy.

5. Dhokala

step-1:Pour approx 2-3 cups water in steamer at bottom and heat it over medium flame.make sure that steamer is at least 4-5 minutes heated before placing the plates.grease plates using oil.

step-2:Take all ingredients like 1cup gram flour(Besan),1 spoon sugar,half lemon,1 spoon solt,50 gm oil,1 tablespoon semilina(rava),1 spoon crushed chilli-ginger paste,2-3 spoon curd in bowl.

step-3:Mix them properly into smooth batter.make sure that there is no lump.

step-4:Add 1 teaspoon fruit solt(eno) in batter and stir in one direction for approximately 1 minute.you will notice its size would increase almost double.

step-5:Now,powr the batter immediately into each greased plate and fill upto 1/2 inch tickness.

step-6:place plates in stremer and steam for 10-15 minutes over medium flame.

step-7:after 10-15 minutes,insert a knife or toothpick into Dhokala and check if it comes out clean.if it does ,then it is ready overwise cook 2-3 minutes more.

step-8:Takeout plates from steamer and let them cool for few minutes.cut fluffy khaman dhokala into small square using knife.

step-9:Tempering:
heat 2 spoon oil in a small pan and add 1/2 spoon mustard seeds and pinch of hing when seeds begin to crackle,add 1/2 spoon cumin seeds 10-15 curry leaves and green chillies.saute them for few seconds.
Add 1/3 cup water and 1 spoon sugar and bring it to a boil.let it cook for minute over high flame.Tempering is ready then pour it over dhokala and toss gently until each dhokala is well coated with Tempering.

step-10:Garnish with 2 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves and 2 tablespoon grated coconut.and serve with green coriander Chutney.and Enjoy.
