Fish Curry
Step-1 This fish curry is made much the same traditional way by sauteing onions,ginger,garlic,tomatoes and ground spices.later this onion tomato masala is simmered to cook down the ingredients and bring out the flavors.
step-2 Marinated fish is seared on a pan and then cooked brifly in the prepared curry.
Step-3 A lot of people add the fish directly to the simmering curry.but we personally prefer to marinate and sear first as this cuts down the fishy smell.though this step is optional.I highly recomment this if you are new to seafood curries and stews.
Step-4 I prefer to blend the onion tomato masala to give the curry/gravy a thicker texture with superior flavors.this is what makes this dish the best.
Step-5 this recipe uses a small amount of coconut. you can use fresh grated or frozen coconut or coconut milk.if you do not consume coconut,you may simply leave out that subtitute with cashews.
Step-6 this recipe is easily adoptable and you can play around with the incredients.if you love tamarind in your fish curry, feel free to add some while you make the curry.you also squeeze some lemon juice before serving.to use amchur, add it at the finishing stage.
Chicken Curry
step-1 Heat 2 tablespoon of oil in heavy bottom pan.when the oil turns hot add 1 small bay leaf,2 inch cinnamon piece,4 cloves,3 green cardamoms.
step-2 Add 1 cup very finely chopped onions and 1 chopped green chilli pepper.Saute the onions till they turn golden.to speed up,I used another tablespoon of oil here and this is optional .
step-3 Add 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste.make sure the onions turn golden before adding ginger garlic paste.Saute ginger for about 2 to 3 minutes or until raw smell disappears completly.
step-4 Add 1/2 cup tomatoes also add 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder and 1/2 teaspoon salt.nest fry until the tomatoes turn completly mushy and the raw smell has gone.
step-5 Add 1 teaspoon red chilli powder and 1/4 cup whisked(curd).to substitude yogurt,you can soak 12 chachew nuts or almonds or 3 tablespoon white poppy seeds in 1/2 cup hot water for 30 minutes.blend it to a smooth paste and add here.
step-6 Saute on a medium flame until the mixture becomes thick and begins to smell good.Add 1/2 kg checken,1 teaspoon garam masala powder,1 teaspoon coriander powder,2 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves or mint leaves.
step-8 Saute for 3 to 4 minutes or until the chicken turns pale.Cover and cook on a low flame for about 3 to 4 minute so the chicken absorbs the flavors.meamwhile,heat 1 cup water in a separate pot.you can also microwave the water in a cup for 1 minutes .
step-9 Pour 1/2 to 3/4 cup hot water or as needed.using cold water often makes the meat tough and hard.Make sure you use just enough water to partially cover the chicken.Cover and cook on a low to medium heat until soft tender.it took about 10 minuts.this may vary depending on the age of the chicken or the size of the pieces.do not cook on high flame temprature.
step-9 Lastly check whether the chicken is done by pricking it with a fork.it must be soft and should fall off the bone easily.taste the curry and add more salt if need.also add 1/2 teaspoon more garam masala if you feel the curry is low on flavor.
step-10 When the curry reaches a desired consistency switch off the stove.add some coriander leaves for garnish.keep covered until you serve.
step-11 Serve chicken curry with rice,turmeric rice,jeera rice,ghee rice,naan or chapati.also have a simple raita and onion salad or kachmber to go on the side.
Chicken Fry
step-1 Take garlic,ginger and curry leaves in a blender.grind to a coarse puree without adding water.
step-2 Take chilli powder,coriander powder,cumin powder,pepper powder,garam masala powder,turmeric powder and salt in a bowl.add the ground garlic and curry leaves puree.add lemon juice or vinegar,all purpose flour.rice flour and mix well.
step-3 Add water and make it into a thick paste.add chicken cubes .this is boneless chicken thighs and mix well.
step-4 Add some red food colour.this step is optional.
step-5 Mix well.leave this to marinate for 1 to 2 hours.
step-6 then deep fry chicken pieces in a hot oil.cook it till golden brown.
step-7 then drain them and serve
Chicken Biryani
step-1 Heat ghee in a pan,and add cinnamon,cloves,green cardamon,star anise,whole peppercorns and black cardamom.
step-2 then add onions and saute till golden brown.
step-3 Add the ginder-garlic paste and saite till the raw smell is gone.add the red chilli powder,tirmeric powder and coriander powder and saute for a minute.
step-4 Add tomatoes and saute till they are well-cooked.then,add chicken pieces and saute for 5 minutes.
step-5 Add water,crumbled maggy magic cubes(chicken),mint leavs.mix well and add salt to taste.add the rice when the water start boil.
step-6 Lower the flame,cover and cook till the rice and chicken are done.
step-7 Serve with raita and enjoy.
Kolambi fry
step-1 Take kolambi in a bowl.
step-2 Add ginger-garlic paste,turmeric powder,salt,red chilli powder,and lemon juice.
step-3 Mix well and marinate it for about 10-15 minutes.
step-4 Heat up oil in a pan add onion and fry until it gets nice golden color for about 4-5 minutes.add ginger-garlic paste,red chilli powder,malvani masala,coriander powder and salt.you can add garam masala instate of malvani masala.Mix well and fry for about 2-3 minutes.
step-5 Add marinated kolambi and mix well.it should get nice coating of spices.
step-6 Sprinkle a little water over the kolambi so that the masala will not stick to the pan.
step-7 cover and cook on medium flame for maximum 4-5 minutes.do not overcook Kolambi.
step-8 Turn off the gas and Kolambi fry is ready.Kolambi fry goes well with rice Bhakari.also it can be served with rice as a side dish.
Sukat Fry
step-1 Trim off the unwanted parts of the dry prawns.dry roast the prowns in a pan for 1-2 minuts.clean,wash,rinse and soak in water for 10 minutes.then drain off excess water and keep it aside for use.
step-2 Heat oil in pan.add ginger,garlic and fry for few minutes.then add onion and fry light brown.
step-3 Add soak prowns and stir fry for 1 minutes.
step-4 Add chilli powder,termeric powder salt and mix well.
step-5 Add tomato and coriander leaves and cook till tomatoes soften
step-6 Drizzle 2 tablespoon water and mix well.cook for 8-10 minutes on low flame.then open and mix well.
step-7 Off the flame and serve with Paw/Roti/Phulkas/Bhakari and Enjoy.
